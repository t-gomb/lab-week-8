About me

My name is Trent, I don't have any nicknames and some of my hobbies include:

  • Snowboarding
  • backpacking
  • cooking
  • playing video/board games
I spend alot of my free time playing games and exploring different recipes to cook. Typically, I cook Asian food mostly Chinese as I am half-Chinese. I also really enjoy cooking and eating Thai cuisine because I really like the flavour profile it has. My other hobbies like snowboarding and backpacking are very season and weather dependant. My favourite mountain to snowboard at is probably Mt.Baker or Sun Peaks Ski resort, but due to the current restrictions, it makes it very difficult to get there. As for backpacking my favourite trail is still the Howe Sound Crest trail, it's been a long time since I've been backpacking so hopefully I can get out again soon!
